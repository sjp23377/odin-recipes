Description: Cold Cut Sandwich!

Ingredients:
  • Bread
  • Turkey Ham
  • Lettuce
  • Egg
  • Banana
  • Almond Milk
  • Ice Cubes
  • Yogurt (optional)
Seasonings:
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Red Pepper Flakes

::---Start---::

Directions:
  • Toast bread in toaster.
  • Oil pan on medium heat.
  • Pan fry egg. Season with salt, black pepper, red pepper flakes.
  • Place turkey ham and lettuce on toasted bread.
  • Place fried egg on bread.
  • Close sandwich together.

Enjoy!!!

::---End---::
