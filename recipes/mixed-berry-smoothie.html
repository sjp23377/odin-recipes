Description: Mixed Berry Smoothie!

Ingredients:
  • Blueberries
  • Strawberries
  • Spinach
  • Kale
  • Banana
  • Almond Milk
  • Ice Cubes
  • Yogurt (optional)

::---Start---::

Directions:
  • Add all ingredients in blender.
  • Cover and blend on medium to high speed until mixed.

Enjoy!!!

::---End---::
