Description: Homemade Chipotle Chicken Bowl

Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Chicken Breast
  • Lettuce
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Red Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Jalepeno Pepper
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Corn on the cob
Seasonings:
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Dried Oregano
  • Paprika
  • Ground Cumin
  • Garlic Powder

::---Start---::

Prep rice
  1. Rinse rice then cook rice in rice cooker.
  2. Or, cook microwaveable rice while cooking chicken in last step.
Make salsa:
  1. Dice red onion and tomato.
  2. Rougly chop cilantro and jalapeno pepper.
  3. Put red onion, tomato, cilantro, and jalapeno in a bowl.
  4. Squeeze lime onto bowl.
  5. Season with salt, black pepper, dried oregano, and ground cumin.
  6. Mix with spoon.
  7. Set aside.
Prep Corn
  1. Microwave corn for 2 minutes.
  2. Cut corn off the cob with knife.
Parcook fajitas
  1. Seasonings: Salt, Black Pepper, Dried Oregano.
  2. Cut red onion and green bell pepper lengthwise.
  3. Oil pan.
  4. Pre-heat pan to medium.
  5. Toss red onion and green bell pepper with seasonings until before soft.
Cook chicken and fajitas:
  1. Seasonings: Salt, Black Pepper, Paprika, Garlic Powder.
  2. Cut chicken with scissors to your preference.
  3. Oil pan.
  4. Pre-heat pan to medium.
  5. Pan fry chicken.
  6. Season chicken while cooking.
  7. Add fajitas and corn when chicken is almost done then mix.
Mix all ingredients in a bowl

Enjoy!!!

::---End---::
